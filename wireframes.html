Basic Information

Professionally validated information about HIV.

What happens after an HIV diagnosis?
HIV is a manageable chronic condition with treatment. With proper medical care, people with HIV live long and healthy lives.
Is treatment effective?
Yes. Antiretroviral treatment (ART) is highly effective. With treatment adherence, the viral load can become undetectable, which means the virus cannot be transmitted (Undetectable = Untransmittable).
Where to get professional help in Costa Rica?
CCSS (Caja Costarricense de Seguro Social) offers free treatment for people with HIV. You can go to your local clinic or request a referral. ‚Üí CCSS website
What is the window period?
It's the time between HIV exposure and when a test can detect the virus. Generally 2-4 weeks, but can take up to 3 months in some cases.
